## The 6 engagement archetypes (proven in-niche)
1. **Rule-change alert** — "EES is live / ETIAS lands Q4 2026". Timely, authority, shareable.
2. **Refusal listicle** — "N reasons Schengen visas get refused". High saves; evergreen.
3. **Refusal story** — "I got refused. Here's the one line that did it." Drives comments.
4. **Myth-bust** — "ETIAS is NOT a visa." Shares + corrects a common error.
5. **Scam warning** — "Anyone selling ETIAS now is a scam." Protective = shares.
6. **Chaos reaction** — "14-hour border queues." News-reactive, short shelf life.
