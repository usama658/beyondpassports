# Long-stay (Type D) visa research — UK market

Research date: 2026-07-03. Source: Ahrefs Keywords Explorer (volumes = avg monthly
searches; KD = keyword difficulty 0-100; global = worldwide sum). UK = country `gb`.

## Terminology
- **Schengen visa** = short-stay, Type C, ≤90 days (what the site does today).
- **Long-stay visa** = national, **Type D**, country-specific residence/work/study
  routes. A D-visa still permits Schengen-area travel while you are resident.
- Audience for long-stay: post-Brexit UK nationals moving to the EU (and, if pages
  are not geo-restricted, the much larger global applicant pool).

## Compliance (applies to every long-stay page)
- Long-stay/residence advice is **regulated legal advice** in most EU states
  (e.g. Germany's Rechtsdienstleistungsgesetz). "Registered in Germany" ≠ licensed
  to advise.
- Safe model: **preparation + assistance + self-service tools** (checklists,
  calculators, form-filling, appointment booking, translation coordination) —
  **not** personalised eligibility/legal opinions.
- For the advice layer and for golden/investor visas, **partner a licensed
  immigration lawyer**; do not sell prep for investor routes.
- No visa/approval guarantee anywhere (DMCCA). Consulate/authority decides.

## Germany long-stay (deduped)
"Digital nomad visa Germany", "German freelance visa" and "Freiberufler visa" are all
the **same §21 self-employment/freelance route** — one page, not three. Germany has
**no dedicated digital-nomad visa**. "Chancenkarte" = "Opportunity Card" (same thing),
points-based job-seeker visa launched **June 2024**.

## Reads
- **UK-only demand is modest** (hundreds/mo). The big 15-25k volumes are **global**
  (India, Nigeria, Pakistan, US source countries), not UK. Write UK-focused copy but
  **do not geo-restrict** — the same pages catch global traffic.
- **Two money themes for UK: Spain + Portugal** — retirees + remote workers moving
  south post-Brexit.
- **Winnable cluster (KD 3-12):** Spain Digital Nomad, Spain Non-Lucrative, Portugal
  D7, Portugal D8, Italy Digital Nomad, Greece Digital Nomad.
- **Golden/investor visas** = big volume, WRONG product for a prep service (investment
  thresholds, heavy legal regulation, Spain's is dead). Cover informational + **refer**.
- Operationally coherent: UK residents lodge national D-visas at the destination's
  consulate **in the UK** (VFS/TLS) — same appointment + document model as Schengen.
