## Realism tips (Gemini / Imagen)

- Vary lighting per person — identical light across all 4 reads as generated.
- Candid, slightly off-centre framing. Dead-centre = AI tell.
- One imperfection each (stray hair, slight squint, uneven smile).
- If negatives are supported: `--no cartoon, 3d render, cgi, plastic skin`.
