# Competitor Analysis — Rejection-focused point-of-sale

**Purpose:** Teardown of rival visa/eVisa services to inform Beyond Passports' rejection
proposition (Re-Application Promise + rejection content silo). Living doc — append each
competitor as we go.

**Method:** one competitor at a time → page by page → section by section. Same grid everywhere.

**Compliance frame (ours, non-negotiable):** no approval-rate %, no fabricated stats, no
"guaranteed approval", "rejection-proof" only as "we remove the avoidable causes"; always
"independent service · not a government website · the decision is the authority's."

1. ✅ **getbrazilvisa.com** — niche, lawyer-led (COMPLETE, 9 pages)

# 1. getbrazilvisa.com — COMPLETE

**Model in one line:** named OAB lawyer (authority) + refusal-cause loss-aversion content +
a safety-net offer at the money-page buy button + a strong "do you need a lawyer?" objection
page + bylined SEO spokes. Wide-but-shallow silo; **no published terms; no compliance disclaimers.**

**Pricing/offer:** Quick Double-Check **$250** (review only, you submit) · Full Service **$599**
(we submit + *"Free resubmission if rejected"* + *"rejection-proof templates"*). Anchored vs
"lawyers typically ~$1,200".

## Page A — Home
- Hero: *"We prepare rejection-proof applications. Documents validated, forms filled correctly,
  ready to submit."* (specificity) · "Reply within 2h · No commitment".
- Who-we-are: **Camila, OAB/CE 50.065, "specializes exclusively"** (authority + badges).
- Approach: *"we've processed enough applications to know exactly what gets approved, and what
  gets flagged."*
- Pricing T2: *"~$1,200 typically"* anchor + **"Free resubmission if rejected"** (risk-reversal at CTA).
- Footer "Official Resources": links to gov immigration portals (authority + non-impersonation).
- **Steal:** rejection-in-hero; named lead + credential on home; safety-net badge at CTA; gov-links block.

## Page B — Money `/brazil-digital-nomad-visa`
- **★ "6 Common Document Mistakes That Cause Denials"** — *"Insufficient income proof… (35% of
  rejections)"* (loss-aversion + specificity). **Their single most transplantable, highest-converting block.**
- "Quick Answers": *"DIY ~85% / 3–6 months vs specialist 95%+ / 30 days"* (comparison).
- "We handle complex cases… direct attorney Camila… no paralegals, no ticket systems."
- "Our Application Data: 50+ visas" — *"95%+ vs ~85%, 28 days"* (numerical authority).
- Final CTA badge: *"💳 Secure payment · 🔒 Money-back guarantee · ⭐ 5.0"*.
- **Steal:** the refusal-causes→our-check section; DIY-vs-us (qualitative); Promise badge at CTA;
  "what if refused?" FAQ. **Avoid:** the 95%/85% + per-cause %.

## Page C — About/authority `/camila-araujo-mota`
- *"One visa category, mastered completely."* (niche = authority)
- **OAB/CE 50.065 + a direct verification link to the OAB registry** (proof, kills skepticism — strongest move).
- *"No junior associates, no templates handed off to non-lawyers, no outsourced document prep."* /
  *"When Camila's name appears, she has read every page of your file."* (personal accountability).
- *"an uncommon data advantage: she knows which document presentations get approved on the first submission."*
- Photo + OAB badge overlay; CTAs: "Speak with Camila on WhatsApp" / "Verify license on OAB"; 2-hr SLA.
- **Steal:** named + verifiable UK case-lead; "reads every page, nothing outsourced"; honest flip
  "we know why applications get refused, and check for each." **#1 trust lever overall.**
- **Hard dependency:** requires a REAL named person + credential (can't fabricate).

## Page D — "Do you need a lawyer?" `/brazil-digital-nomad-visa-lawyer` (best objection page)
- *"No, a lawyer is not legally required… but the most common rejection reasons are preventable
  with professional review."* — concedes DIY is possible, then weaponises refusal data.
- Refusal breakdown: *"missing apostilles (34%), insufficient income docs (28%), expired health
  insurance (19%)" — "based on 50+ applications".*
- **"When should you get professional help?"** scenario-matcher (complex income / families /
  first-timers / time-sensitive → "Professional recommended").
- **Real Reddit/forum quotes** with upvotes + thread links (third-party social proof).
- **Steal:** own this page type — honest "Do you need help with your UK visa?" led by refusal data
  + a should-you-get-help matcher + real public-forum proof.

## Page E — Spoke `/uk-citizen-digital-nomad-visa-brazil`
- Per-segment structure (requirements → DBS → apostille → routes → costs → tax → FAQ).
- **Bylined (Camila, OAB + registry link) + "Reviewed June 2026" freshness.**
- Dual CTA woven through: free WhatsApp consult + paid tiers.
- Rejection-thin (only timing warnings). **We beat by leading spokes with refusal causes.**
- **Steal:** case-lead byline + "Reviewed {date}" on every spoke; dual CTA woven through content.

## Page F — "Which visa do I need?" `/which-brazil-visa-do-i-need`
- Static if-then decision tree (NOT interactive — we beat with a real checker).
- **Honest eligibility gate:** *"if none of the above applies, you do not currently qualify for any
  long-stay visa."* (trust + filters bad-fit leads).
- "Common mistakes" with legal cites + overstay penalty warning (loss-aversion).
- Hand-off: *"the attorney-reviewed intake at /apply takes 10 minutes."*
- **Steal:** honest "you may not qualify" gate; "mistakes that get you refused/banned" framing.

## Page G — Apply `/apply`
- Bare: Name/Email/Phone + Submit; OAB credential + phone + *"All information is confidential."*
- **NO guarantee/refund/Promise wording at the point of purchase** — their safety net never
  appears at the buy step. **Clear opening: we badge the Promise at apply/checkout.**

## Page H — Blog/Guides hub `/blog`
- 6 thematic categories, bylined, hub last-modified 2026-06-18.
- **ZERO standalone rejection/denial/appeal posts** — nobody owns the rejection cluster.
- **★ Best interlinking move:** one *"Apostille Guide — US/UK/CA/AU/DE/FR + the #1 mistake that
  causes visa rejections"* post captures 6+ geo long-tail clusters from a single page.
- **Steal:** a per-country "refusal reasons" pillar for geo long-tail; the whole rejection cluster.

## Page I — Privacy/Terms/Refund `/privacy`
- **Refund policy NOT published** (link → contact). Terms link → broken anchor. **No "not a
  government site" / "outcome not guaranteed" disclaimers.**
- Their "95% / free resubmission / money-back" = marketing claims, **not contractual terms.**
- **Their biggest credibility hole — we win with real published `/legal#re-application-promise` + disclaimers.**

**Hard input needed:** a real named UK authority + credential.

  "why visas get refused" → "how we rejection-proof your application" (eligibility → AI check #99 →

  two columns — each common cause (from taxonomy #73) → the exact check that catches it. Honest:

  Here's what actually gets applications refused — and what we catch before submission." +
