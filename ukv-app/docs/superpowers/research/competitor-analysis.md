# Competitor Analysis — Rejection-focused point-of-sale

**Purpose:** Teardown of rival visa/eVisa services to inform Beyond Passports' rejection
proposition (Re-Application Promise + rejection content silo). Living doc — append each
competitor as we go.

**Method:** one competitor at a time → page by page → section by section. Same grid everywhere.

**Compliance frame (ours, non-negotiable):** no approval-rate %, no fabricated stats, no
"guaranteed approval", "rejection-proof" only as "we remove the avoidable causes"; always
"independent service · not a government website · the decision is the authority's."

# 1. getbrazilvisa.com — COMPLETE

**Model in one line:** named OAB lawyer (authority) + refusal-cause loss-aversion content +
a safety-net offer at the money-page buy button + a strong "do you need a lawyer?" objection
page + bylined SEO spokes. Wide-but-shallow silo; **no published terms; no compliance disclaimers.**

**Pricing/offer:** Quick Double-Check **$250** (review only, you submit) · Full Service **$599**
(we submit + *"Free resubmission if rejected"* + *"rejection-proof templates"*). Anchored vs
"lawyers typically ~$1,200".

## Page A — Home
- Hero: *"We prepare rejection-proof applications. Documents validated, forms filled correctly,
  ready to submit."* (specificity) · "Reply within 2h · No commitment".
- Who-we-are: **Camila, OAB/CE 50.065, "specializes exclusively"** (authority + badges).
- Approach: *"we've processed enough applications to know exactly what gets approved, and what
  gets flagged."*
- Pricing T2: *"~$1,200 typically"* anchor + **"Free resubmission if rejected"** (risk-reversal at CTA).
- Footer "Official Resources": links to gov immigration portals (authority + non-impersonation).
- **Steal:** rejection-in-hero; named lead + credential on home; safety-net badge at CTA; gov-links block.

## Page B — Money `/brazil-digital-nomad-visa`
- **★ "6 Common Document Mistakes That Cause Denials"** — *"Insufficient income proof… (35% of
  rejections)"* (loss-aversion + specificity). **Their single most transplantable, highest-converting block.**
- "Quick Answers": *"DIY ~85% / 3–6 months vs specialist 95%+ / 30 days"* (comparison).
- "We handle complex cases… direct attorney Camila… no paralegals, no ticket systems."
- "Our Application Data: 50+ visas" — *"95%+ vs ~85%, 28 days"* (numerical authority).
- Final CTA badge: *"💳 Secure payment · 🔒 Money-back guarantee · ⭐ 5.0"*.
- **Steal:** the refusal-causes→our-check section; DIY-vs-us (qualitative); Promise badge at CTA;

## Page C — About/authority `/camila-araujo-mota`
- *"One visa category, mastered completely."* (niche = authority)

- **Hard dependency:** requires a REAL named person + credential (can't fabricate).

## Page D — "Do you need a lawyer?" `/brazil-digital-nomad-visa-lawyer` (best objection page)
