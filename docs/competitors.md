## Beyond Passports positioning deltas (white space)
- **Prevention-led brand** — rivals say "avoid mistakes" generically; none own refusal-prevention as the core promise.
- **Transparent fixed fees** — most hide pricing; only Scott's (£250+VAT) and Aviva (£350–435) shown publicly.
- **Honest no-approval-guarantee** — safer than rivals' "100% refund / high approval" claims (UK DMCCA 2024 + no one controls a government decision).
- **Bundle small agents skip:** tracked passport return, itineraries, FCA-safe insurance signpost, live status tracker, biometrics guidance — packaged premium.
