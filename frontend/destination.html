Questions

Turkey eVisa FAQ

Do I actually need a Turkey eVisa?

Many travellers do, but it depends on your nationality, passport and trip. Tell us your details and we'll confirm whether you need one before you pay — and we'll tell you honestly if you don't.

How long does it take?

Typical processing is around 1–3 business days for our handling. Express prioritises your case within our team. Note that the government sets its own processing time, which we can't control or shorten.

Is this the official government website?

No. UKVisaCo is an independent service — we are not a government website and not affiliated with any government. We prepare, check and submit your application on your behalf for a separate service fee.

What does your fee cover, and is the government fee included?

Our fee covers our preparation, checking, submission and support. The Turkish government charges its own separate fee for the eVisa, which is shown clearly before you pay. The two are always kept separate.

What happens if my application is refused?

The outcome of any application is decided solely by the Turkish authorities — we cannot guarantee approval. We focus on getting your application accurate and complete to give it the best chance, and we're upfront with you throughout.
